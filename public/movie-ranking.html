The Best Movies by Jeremiah Lopez

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Hereditary

This is my one of my favorite modern horror movies of all time. It is truly a scary experience!

The Shining

This is considered one of the best movies of all time, and for good reason!

Kill Bill Vol. 1

Truly a masterpiece in action. So much fun to watch with friends.

The Blair Witch Project

One of the most popular found footage films of all time, and it is a really good one too.

Alien

The first movie of the franchise has proven to be a truly fantastic film that still holds up today!
